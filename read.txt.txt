Restaurant Management System Documentation

1.Introduction
The Restaurant Management System (RMS) is a web application developed using Django. It is designed to streamline restaurant operations, including managing reservations, menu items, orders, and staff. This documentation provides a comprehensive guide on how to use, configure, and extend the system.
